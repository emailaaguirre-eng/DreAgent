# OpenAI Model Recommendations by Mode

Based on OpenAI's model capabilities and each mode's requirements:

## Model Capabilities (from OpenAI documentation):

### GPT-4o
- **Best for**: General purpose, fast responses, good balance
- **Strengths**: Fast, cost-effective, good for most tasks
- **Use cases**: General chat, coding, analysis

### GPT-4o-mini
- **Best for**: Simple tasks, high volume, cost-sensitive
- **Strengths**: Very fast, very cheap
- **Use cases**: Simple Q&A, basic tasks

### GPT-4 Turbo
- **Best for**: Complex reasoning, analysis, research
- **Strengths**: Better reasoning than GPT-4o, good for complex tasks
- **Use cases**: Research, analysis, complex problem solving

### GPT-4
- **Best for**: Maximum accuracy, complex reasoning
- **Strengths**: Best reasoning capabilities
- **Use cases**: Critical accuracy needs, complex analysis

### GPT-3.5 Turbo
- **Best for**: Simple tasks, high volume
- **Strengths**: Fast, cheap
- **Use cases**: Basic chat, simple tasks

## Mode Analysis:

### 1. General Assistant & Triage
- **Task**: Simple routing, general questions, welcoming
- **Requirements**: Fast response, friendly tone, basic understanding
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4o-mini** or **GPT-4o** (fast, good enough for routing)
- **Reasoning**: Doesn't need complex reasoning, just needs to understand intent and route

### 2. IT Support
- **Task**: Coding, debugging, technical troubleshooting
- **Requirements**: Code understanding, accuracy, technical knowledge
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4o** or **GPT-4 Turbo** (good code understanding)
- **Reasoning**: Needs good code comprehension and generation

### 3. Executive Assistant & Operations
- **Task**: Task management, screen automation, professional communication
- **Requirements**: Instruction following, task understanding, precision
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4o** (good instruction following, fast)
- **Reasoning**: Needs to understand and execute tasks accurately

### 4. Incentives & Client Forms
- **Task**: Research grants, credits, form handling
- **Requirements**: Research capabilities, information synthesis
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4 Turbo** (better research and analysis)
- **Reasoning**: Needs to research and synthesize information

### 5. Research & Learning
- **Task**: Learning, research, explanations
- **Requirements**: Deep understanding, clear explanations, research
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4 Turbo** (better reasoning for complex topics)
- **Reasoning**: Needs to break down complex topics clearly

### 6. Legal Research & Drafting
- **Task**: Legal research, accuracy critical, complex reasoning
- **Requirements**: Maximum accuracy, zero hallucinations, complex reasoning
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4 Turbo** or **GPT-4** (best reasoning, most accurate)
- **Reasoning**: Legal work requires highest accuracy and reasoning

### 7. Finance & Tax
- **Task**: Financial accuracy, tax guidance, calculations
- **Requirements**: High accuracy, careful analysis
- **Best Model**: **GPT-4 Turbo** (better accuracy than GPT-4o)
- **Reasoning**: Financial matters require precision

## Recommended Defaults:

1. **General Assistant & Triage**: GPT-4o-mini (fast, sufficient for routing)
2. **IT Support**: GPT-4o (good code understanding, fast)
3. **Executive Assistant**: GPT-4o (good instruction following)
4. **Incentives & Client Forms**: GPT-4 Turbo (better research)
5. **Research & Learning**: GPT-4 Turbo (better explanations)
6. **Legal Research**: GPT-4 Turbo (best accuracy and reasoning)
7. **Finance & Tax**: GPT-4 Turbo (better accuracy)
